About the Program

M-Powered is a four-day corporate training initiative by Rajagiri College of Social Sciences in association with Vertical Eye. The program is designed to shape postgraduates into confident and corporate-ready individuals through interactive workshops, personal transformation sessions, and practical exposure.

Meet the Trainers

Krish Sir and Mridula Mam

Krish Sir and Mridula Mam are award-winning corporate trainers and internationally certified life skills coaches. With extensive experience as motivational speakers, psychologists, entrepreneurs, and authors, they have inspired and trained over 60,000 participants worldwide. Their mission is to empower individuals and positively transform lives through impactful coaching and training.
